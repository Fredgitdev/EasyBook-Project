Frequently Asked Questions

Basic Questions
1. What is EasyBook?

EasyBook is an online booking system that allows passengers to conveniently reserve bus tickets for their travel. It is designed specifically for the public transport sector, making it easier for users to plan their journeys and book tickets in advance.

2. How does EasyBook work?

EasyBook allows users to search for available buses, select their departure and arrival points, choose travel dates, and make secure payments for their tickets—all from the comfort of their devices.

3. Do I need to create an account to book a ticket?

Creating an account on EasyBook offers the advantage of tracking your bookings and managing payment methods.

Other Questions
4. Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes, you can book tickets for someone else by entering their details during the booking process.

5. How do I check the availability of buses?

Simply enter your departure and arrival locations, select your desired travel date, and EasyBook will show you a list of available buses, along with timings and prices.

6. Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, EasyBook allows users to modify or cancel their bookings. However, cancellation and modification policies depend on the bus company and the specific ticket type, so please check the terms before making changes.
